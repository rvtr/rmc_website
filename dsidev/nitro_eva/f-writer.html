--- layout: devinfo app: Nitro_EVA_F-WRITER redirect_from: /dsidev/nitro_eva_f-writer.html preview: /preview.png --- {% include nitro_eva_commonality.html %} F-WRITER is the Firmware writer for the DS. Each version contains a single copy of the firmware in nitro:/Ipl2/link/. This firmware has a FF filled header, and no config region. Strings in copies of F-WRITER have been found to list undumped and unknown firmware versions. Unlike the other Nitro EVAs, F-WRITER is in its own special category. The bundled tests are exclusive to F-WRITER, and it does not contain any of the standard tests.
