--- layout: devinfo app: Nitro_EVA_TP_JIG redirect_from: /dsidev/nitro_eva_tp_jig.html preview: /preview.png --- {% include nitro_eva_commonality.html %} TP_JIG is like TP_ACCURACY, but made for very precise touch screen testing.

The DS would be placed into a factory jig with fine points that press down on the touch screen. This setup would make sure that the screen is calibrated within very fine tolerances (<5 px).
