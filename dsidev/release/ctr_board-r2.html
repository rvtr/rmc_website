And an anonymous uploader has just sent in a new CTR BOARD! This tool would have been used in factories to test 3DS consoles with a DSi. A more detailed explaination of BOARD can be found in our previous release, or the dedicated BOARD page. This specific copy targets CTR (old 3DS), SPR (3DS XL), and FTR (2DS). This is a development signed ROM.

Unlike most TWL EVAs, you cannot access the menu by holding START on boot. The BOARD software automatically tries communicating to an XPort device. Since we do not have the factory hardware needed for communications, the software will be stuck trying to load until it eventually times out. To skip XPort setup, download the "CTR BOARD Config" zip, and extract the contents to the root of the SD. Then open seq_cnfdata.txt, and edit the StartMenu variable in the [[TEST MODE]] section from FALSE to TRUE.

Thanks to DeadlyFoez for dumping the SD card that the config came from.
