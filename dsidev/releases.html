PokeWalker factory tests (2025/12/05)

Thanks to ufo, we just got some PokeWalker test software. The tools, "PHC PRODUCT TEST", use a DS to set up PokeWalkers!


AGING and WRITE SDMC-LNC factory tools (2025/04/19)

New DSi factory cart dumps from March42, one of which was never known to exist before now!


MangaON DSi Update: Build recovered (2025/01/04)

A build of the cancelled digital manga service called MangaON has been recovered by MrPinball64! This was assumed to be overwritten in our old release, but it was just fragmented!


X4, X5, and X6 Prototype DSis (2024/12/31)

3 retail prototypes from Voodooween, all running factory firmware but with one new firmware as well!


DSi factory cartridges (2024/12/25)

27 factory cart dumps from j4m13c0, Mecasacax, many of which are firmware installers that have never seen the light of day!


SDMC Launcher DSi (2024/11/01)

A retail DSi from the factory that is still running the unique SDMC Launcher bootloader. Thanks to BrolyBroMan for sharing!


X6 DSi and factory firmwares (2024/10/09)

An X6 prototype DSi and 3 final retail motherboards with factory (prototype) firmware. Huge thanks to MarioDS64 for sharing his prototype, HudsonU for his factory firmware, and Kelly for helping me get ahold of my two boards.


Classroom + MangaON DSi dev NANDs (2023/09/29)

8 NAND dumps from various DSi development units (and 2 retail), some of which were used for developing the Classroom DSi and MangaON. There is also a development cartridge containing the KART, a possible augmented reality test.
Many thanks to collection_nintendo on Instagram for providing the dumps.


X4 Prototype released (2023/05/22)

Lots of detailed photos and the differences of a newly discovered X4 prototype DS lite. Huge thanks to lasscat and ProtonX!
